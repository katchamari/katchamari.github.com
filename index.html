About me

My name is Momo and I'm an art new media student at American River College. After I get my Associate's at the end of this spring I will be going into computer science and I hope to transfer to CSU Monterey. I have a huge interest in graphic design history, but in terms of creating, I really moreso enjoy the development side of things. I love being able to figure out a challenge, even when I have limited knowledge, and it's very rewarding to see the end result of my work.

I usually develop from a mobile first perspective and I love using flexbox to make responsive sites. Feel free to look at what I've created and/or contact me if you have anything to say!
